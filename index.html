IDH 3035 — Final Project Showcase

Honors Seminar VI: Design Thinking. Eight student teams spent the semester applying human-centered design to the Honors College student experience. These are their final solutions.

Each team identified a real challenge facing Honors students — housing, community, study spaces, balancing work and school, the freshman transition — built empathy maps and personas around their user, and prototyped a solution. Click any video to watch their final pitch.
